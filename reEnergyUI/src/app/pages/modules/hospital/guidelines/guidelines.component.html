GUIDANCE NOTES

  1. Fees to be paid through online/bank transfer using this technology portal only. Fees will be calculated based on the number of sanctioned beds and the fee paid by the organisation will be non-refundable.

  2. The application fee plus annual fee mentioned in the above table(s) does not include expenses on travel, lodging or boarding of assessors in case of physical assessment, which will be borne by the applicant organisation on an actual basis.

  3. The certification once granted will be valid for two years. The organisation may apply for renewal as per NABH policy.

  4. The application fee plus a two-year annual fee is payable before Desktop Assessment and after submission of the application form on the technology portal.

  5. NABH may call for an unannounced visit, which could be a Surprise Assessment or based on any concern or any serious incident reported upon by any individual or organisation or media.

  6. Please refer to the NABH certification timeline on the NABH website.

  7. The application should be completed and submitted within 30 days from the date of registration. The application will be deactivated if not completed within the timelines and the organisation will have to reapply.

  8. The number of days is calculated as per the calendar days and includes weekends and weekdays.

  9. All the Questions in the Application Form are Mandatory.

  10. The documents that are uploaded by the organisation as evidence must be clear and readable.

  11. The organisation can log in only on one browser at a time.

  12. The user must be connected to an active internet connection while uploading documents to avoid any type of data loss.

  13. The details entered by the organisation in the application form cannot be edited once submitted (except the details of the certification officer).

  14. The organisation has to ensure that the details are filled accurately with appropriate documents.

  15. The concealing of information or intentionally filling in the wrong information may lead to the cancellation of Registration.

  16. The application fee will be forfeited if the application is rejected.

  17. The following file formats can be uploaded:

    1. Image – JPG, JPEG, PNG
    2. Document - PDF
APPLICATION FORM
A. Under General Information: 

  1. In Split locations - This pertains to all units which are a part of the organisation for example - outreach clinics, etc. 

  2. In Operational Beds - please state the number of beds currently in operation. For example, the hospital may have approval for 250 beds (called sanctioned beds) but presently if only 100 beds are operational, please mention only 100. However, the hospital shall inform NABH of any increase in operational beds within 15 days of making the additional operational beds 

B. Under the Scope of services: : 

  1. Please indicate “Yes” only if at least one consultant is holding a recognized degree managing a single department. Please ensure that there are OPD & IPD services (if applicable) for all the ticked specialities.

    Note - Provision of Emergency Services in concerned departments shall be available (if applicable).

  2. Please note that this list of specialities is based on the recognized AYUSH medical courses by the Central Council of Indian Medicine/ Central Council of Homoeopathy. 

    Note - The scope of certification shall be transcribed as per the filled online application ONLY.  

C. Under Statutory Compliance : 

  1. In the detail of licenses, please select licenses applicable to the organization for example - Clinical Establishment Act, Shops and Establishments Registration Act, etc. Please mention if any license is expired and details as to how it is being addressed. Please upload the supporting documents in the attachment.
